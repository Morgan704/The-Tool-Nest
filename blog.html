Hashtags categorize your content, making it discoverable to users interested in those topics. They're essentially a content discovery tool that can:

QR Code Best Practices: How to Create and Use Them Effectively

September 4, 2025

QR codes have made a huge comeback in recent years, becoming an essential tool for businesses and marketers. When implemented correctly, they can bridge the gap between physical and digital experiences.

What Makes a Good QR Code?

An effective QR code is more than just a random pattern of black squares. It should be:

  • Scannable from a reasonable distance
  • Placed in context with clear instructions
  • Connected to a mobile-optimized destination
  • Tested across multiple devices and scanning apps
