How to Use Our Hashtag Maker to Boost Your Social Media Engagement

September 5, 2025

Hashtags are a powerful tool for increasing your social media visibility and engagement. When used correctly, they can help you reach a wider audience, connect with like-minded users, and boost your content's performance.

Why Hashtags Matter

Hashtags categorize your content, making it discoverable to users interested in those topics. They're essentially a content discovery tool that can:

  • Increase your post's reach beyond your followers
  • Attract targeted followers interested in your niche
  • Boost engagement rates through relevant discovery
  • Help you track campaigns and user-generated content

Best Practices for Hashtag Use

Our hashtag maker tool simplifies the process of finding the perfect hashtags for your content. Here's how to make the most of it:

  • Use a mix of popular and niche-specific hashtags
  • Include 5-11 hashtags per post for optimal engagement
  • Research hashtags before using them to ensure relevance
  • Create branded hashtags for your business or campaigns

Platform-Specific Tips

Each social media platform has its own hashtag culture:

  • Instagram: Use up to 30 hashtags (but 5-11 is optimal)
  • Twitter: Stick to 1-2 hashtags per tweet for best results
  • TikTok: Use 3-5 trending and niche hashtags
  • Facebook: 1-2 hashtags tend to perform best

QR Code Best Practices: How to Create and Use Them Effectively

September 4, 2025

QR codes have made a huge comeback in recent years, becoming an essential tool for businesses and marketers. When implemented correctly, they can bridge the gap between physical and digital experiences.

What Makes a Good QR Code?

An effective QR code is more than just a random pattern of black squares. It should be:

  • Scannable from a reasonable distance
  • Placed in context with clear instructions
  • Connected to a mobile-optimized destination
  • Tested across multiple devices and scanning apps

Design Tips for QR Codes

While functionality is key, you can also make your QR codes visually appealing:

  • Add your logo or brand element to the center
  • Use brand colors while maintaining sufficient contrast
  • Incorporate a clear call-to-action around the code
  • Ensure the quiet zone (margin) is preserved

Strategic Implementation

Where and how you use QR codes significantly impacts their effectiveness:

  • Print materials: Business cards, brochures, flyers
  • Product packaging: Link to usage instructions or reviews
  • Restaurant menus: Link to digital menus or ordering systems
  • Retail displays: Provide additional product information
  • Event signage: Connect to schedules or registration pages

Tracking and Analytics

One of the biggest advantages of QR codes is the ability to track engagement. Use our QR code generator to create trackable codes that provide insights into:

  • Number of scans over time
  • Geographical location of users
  • Devices and operating systems used
  • Peak scanning times
