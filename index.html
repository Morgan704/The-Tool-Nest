Tool Tutorials & Guides

Mastering the Pencil Sketch Tool

Transform any photo into a stunning work of art with our advanced client-side sketch tool.

  1. Upload Your Image: Click the upload area and select a high-quality JPG, PNG, or WEBP file from your device.
  2. Adjust Sketch Intensity: Once the sketch appears, use the "Sketch Intensity" slider. Move it to the right for a darker, more defined sketch, or to the left for a lighter, softer effect. The preview updates in real-time.
  3. Download Your Art: When you're satisfied, click the green "Download Sketch" button. Your artwork will be saved as a high-resolution PNG file with a transparent background.

Pro Tip: Images with clear subjects and good contrast produce the best sketch results. Try different intensity levels to find the perfect style for your photo.

How to Effortlessly Merge PDF Files

Combine multiple PDF documents into a single, organized file directly in your browser. No uploads, completely private.

  1. Select Your PDFs: Click the upload area and select two or more PDF files. You can select multiple files at once by holding Ctrl (or Cmd on Mac) while clicking.
  2. Review Your Files: A list of the selected files will appear. The PDFs will be merged in the order they are listed. (Note: Reordering is planned for a future update).
  3. Merge and Download: Click the green "Merge & Download" button. Our tool will process the files instantly and your browser will prompt you to save the new combined PDF.

Pro Tip: If you need to clear your selection and start over, simply click the "Clear" button. This is useful if you select the wrong files.
