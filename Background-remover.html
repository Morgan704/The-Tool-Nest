Tips & Best Practices

Image Quality

Use high-resolution images with clear subject-background contrast for best results.

Best Subjects

People, products, and animals work best. Avoid complex backgrounds or transparent objects.

Post-Processing

Use manual tools for fine-tuning edges and fixing any missed areas.

Format Choice

Save as PNG for transparency, or JPG with solid color background for smaller file sizes.

Processing Time

Client-side processing may take longer but keeps your images private and secure.

Privacy

Client-side processing keeps your images on your device. External tools may store images temporarily.
